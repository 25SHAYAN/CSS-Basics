Colors of the Rainbow

When the rain starts to fall, And the clouds begin to sprawl, Look up high in the sky, A rainbow is passing by. Red, orange, and yellow so bright, Colors that fill us with delight. Green, blue, and purple too, A magical sight, just for you. Like a bridge up above, A symbol of hope, peace, and love. After every storm, it appears, Chasing away all our fears. From one end to the other, Colors blend and hover. A vibrant arc of light, A wondrous and beautiful sight. So let's dance with joy and cheer, As the rainbow draws near. A reminder in the sky, That beauty comes after the cry. The colors of the rainbow so grand, In nature's artwork, hand in hand. A magical promise, shining so, A symbol of hope wherever we go.
